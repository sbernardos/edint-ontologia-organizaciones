Abstract

The purpose of this ontology is to provide a common vocabulary for representing public organizations in Spain, as well as aspects related to organizational charts within those organizations. It is an update of a previous version of the ontology that extended the W3C Organization Ontology. This version adapts that ontology to meet EDINT requirements. The most relevant changes include removing the Office and Economic-Budgetary Management Unit classes, along with their specific properties, and including Spanish Organizacion as a subclass of Organization and edintinf:Facility as a subclass of Site.
